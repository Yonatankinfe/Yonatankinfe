## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate developer based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, blending full-stack engineering with an ever-growing curiosity for Artificial Intelligence. 

* 💼 Currently building efficient, robust systems as a **Junior Software Developer at Tekhaf Trading PLC**.
* 🎓 Pursuing my **Master’s in Computer Science at Admas University**.
* 💻 I specialize in crafting solid backends (PHP, CodeIgniter, Laravel) paired with clean, minimalist user interfaces.
* 🤖 Actively exploring **Machine Learning**, building AI-driven automation bots, and bringing intelligent solutions to life.
* ⚽ When I'm away from the keyboard, I'm likely analyzing a Chelsea FC match, scripting sports content, or unwinding with a good horror or comedy movie.
